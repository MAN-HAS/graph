TREE POSE

Instructions

Benefits
> Get into position

> Tree pose often starts from mountain pose

> both feet planted firmly on the ground and your weight adequately distributed

> so that you are balanced

> Bend one leg at the knee. Choose the leg you are going to fold in first

> Lengthen your body

> Hold and repeat

 > Stretches your feet

> Tree pose can help stretch and strengthen the ligaments and tendons in your feet

> Improves balance

> Tree pose requires proper weight distribution and posture,

which can help provide stability to your groin, thighs, hips, and pelvis

> Strengthens your core.
